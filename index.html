Earn's Burger

Our signature Earn Burger is a plant-based twist on a classic favorite. The patty is made from a blend of organic, locally-sourced vegetables, including carrots, potatoes, and beets. It's topped with a slice of vegan cheese, fresh lettuce, and juicy tomato, all nestled in a soft, handmade bun. Served with a side of crispy fries and our special Earn sauce, this burger is a delicious, guilt-free indulgence.

Price: 9.95 Euro

Earn's Biryani

Our Earn Biryani is a plant-based take on a traditional Indian dish. It features a fragrant blend of basmati rice, slow-cooked with a medley of vegetables and aromatic spices. The dish is topped with a generous helping of crispy fried onions, adding a delicious crunch to every bite. Served with a side of cooling cucumber raita, this dish is a vegan delight for the senses.

Price: 9.95 Euro
